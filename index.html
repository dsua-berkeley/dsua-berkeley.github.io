DATA SCIENCE

Undergraduate Association

The DSUA aims to build community, foster collaboration, and support the undergraduate Data Science student population at UC Berkeley.

Interested in joining?

What is DSUA?

DSUA is open to all students with an interest in data science! There is no formal membership process and you can participate as much or as little as you like. Our goal is to support and connect the undergraduate Data Science community at UC Berkeley with professional development and social events.

Community

We want to build a community of data scientists without the pressure of recruitment. We provide opportunities for you to connect with others, build study groups, and make lifelong friends!

Events

We host student-led activities, career and networking events, skill-building workshops, and opportunities to connect with peers, alumni, and industry professionals.
